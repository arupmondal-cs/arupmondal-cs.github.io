Hello,

Welcome to my webpage!

I'm Arup Mondal, fifth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Ashoka University, advised by Dr. Debayan Gupta. I am also a visiting Ph.D. student (March 2024 - Current) at the Brown University in the Computer Science Department, working under the guidance of Dr. Peihan Miao. My research has also greatly benefited from insightful technical contributions by Dr. Sikhar Patranabis from IBM Research India.

Previously, I was a visiting Ph.D. student (Septermber 2024 - February 2025) at the Technical University of Darmstadt (TU Darmstadt) in the Applied Cryptography Lab, working under the guidance of Dr. Sebastian Faust. Before that, I spent time as a visiting Ph.D. student (November 2023 - September 2024) at the Center for Information Security and Trust (CISAT) at IT University of Copenhagen, where I am advised by Dr. Bernardo David, and I gained valuable research experience as an intern (January 2023 - March 2023) at Technology Innovation Institute (TII) in the Crypto Research Center, working under the supervision of Dr. Abdelrahaman Aly.

I completed my master’s degree in Computer Science at Banaras Hindu University, working with Prof. Swapan Kumar Basu on some aspects of Computer Architecture. My undergraduate degree was in Computer Science from Ramakrishna Mission Residential College, Narendrapur.

For more details, please take a look at my CV.

Research Interests:

My primary area of interest is the theory and design of secure cryptographic schemes for Applied and Verifiable Cryptography. In addition to this, I have a great deal of interest in cryptographic delay functions.
